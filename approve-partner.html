Approve Partner

Create their Firebase account, generate a temp passcode, and send the approval email — all from here.

⚠️ This page writes directly to Firebase. Only use after you've verified the gym owner's documents.

What this does

  1. Generates a random 8-character temporary passcode
  2. Creates a Firebase Auth account with their email + temp passcode
  3. Creates their Firestore user profile with role: "gymPartner" and mustChangePassword: true
  4. Creates a gymPartners doc for their gym dashboard data
  5. Sends them an approval email with the temp passcode and instructions
  6. Updates their application status to approved
